## Overview

Led the strategy and execution of the Meta Local Value Ads (LVA) integration — a product that allows merchants to automatically sync their local inventory data with Meta's advertising infrastructure. This enables hyper-targeted ads that show shoppers nearby product availability, driving both online and foot traffic.

## Problem

Merchants with physical locations and online storefronts struggled to bridge their inventory data with Meta's ad systems. Without real-time product feed connectivity, ads were generic and failed to capitalize on local purchase intent — resulting in wasted ad spend and missed conversion opportunities.

## Approach

- Mapped merchant inventory data schema to Meta's Catalog and Local Inventory Ads format
- Built automated feed sync to keep product availability and pricing current in Meta's system
- Designed an onboarding flow for merchants to connect their catalog and configure LVA campaigns
- Worked cross-functionally with Meta's partnership and ads product teams

## Impact

- Enabled merchants to launch locally targeted Meta ad campaigns with live inventory data
- Drove measurable lift in both online conversions and local store visits for integrated merchants
- Reduced manual ad management overhead through automated catalog syncing
