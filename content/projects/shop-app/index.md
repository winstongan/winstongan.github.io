## Overview

Worked on the partnerships and growth side of Shop App, Shopify's mobile consumer app with tens of millions of shoppers. Focused on helping brands and merchants leverage Shop's discovery and retention features to increase shopper engagement and lifetime value.

## Problem

Despite having a strong merchant base, many brands weren't fully utilizing Shop App's native features — like Shop Pay, Shop Cash, and package tracking — to deepen post-purchase relationships with customers. Awareness and activation among the merchant base was low.

## Approach

- Developed merchant activation playbooks to drive Shop App feature adoption
- Coordinated with Shopify's Shop App product team on partner-facing surface areas
- Built GTM frameworks to help brands communicate Shop App benefits to their customers
- Identified high-potential merchant segments for targeted outreach and enablement

## Impact

- Increased merchant activation rates for Shop App features across a targeted cohort
- Strengthened relationships between Shopify's partner ecosystem and the Shop App team
- Contributed to improved shopper retention metrics for brands leveraging Shop's post-purchase tools
