## Overview

Led the end-to-end development and launch of a Klaviyo integration that connects merchant storefronts to Klaviyo's email marketing and automation platform. The integration enables real-time data sync between customer behavior, purchase events, and Klaviyo flows — unlocking personalized lifecycle marketing at scale.

## Problem

Merchants needed a seamless way to translate storefront activity (product views, cart abandons, purchases) into targeted, automated email campaigns without manual data exports or complex custom development.

## Approach

- Mapped key merchant events and customer attributes to Klaviyo's data model
- Built a real-time event pipeline to sync storefront signals to Klaviyo profiles
- Designed a guided onboarding flow to reduce time-to-first-campaign
- Coordinated closely with Klaviyo's partnership team on certification requirements

## Impact

- Enabled automated lifecycle campaigns for merchants including welcome series, abandoned cart, and post-purchase flows
- Reduced merchant setup time significantly through templated flows and pre-built segments
- Contributed to measurable lift in repeat purchase rates for integrated merchants
