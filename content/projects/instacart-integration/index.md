## Overview

Led the product and partnership strategy for integrating merchant storefronts with Instacart's marketplace. The integration enables merchants to automatically sync product catalogs, pricing, and inventory to Instacart — making their products available for same-day delivery to millions of Instacart shoppers.

## Problem

Merchants interested in same-day delivery faced significant friction setting up on Instacart: manual catalog uploads, inconsistent data formats, and lack of real-time inventory syncing meant listings were often inaccurate and time-consuming to maintain.

## Approach

- Designed a two-way data pipeline between merchant inventory systems and Instacart's catalog API
- Standardized product data mapping to meet Instacart's taxonomy and attribute requirements
- Built real-time inventory sync to minimize out-of-stock issues on the platform
- Partnered with Instacart's business development team on go-to-market and merchant eligibility criteria

## Impact

- Enabled merchants to go live on Instacart with automated catalog management
- Reduced setup time from days to hours through streamlined onboarding
- Opened a new high-intent, same-day delivery revenue channel for integrated merchants
