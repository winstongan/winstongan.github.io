## Overview

Led product and go-to-market strategy for a Shopify app that gives merchants powerful growth tools directly within the Shopify admin. The app streamlines key commerce workflows — from inventory management to customer segmentation — reducing friction and increasing merchant efficiency.

## Problem

Shopify merchants often struggle to coordinate data across multiple tools and platforms. Switching between apps for marketing, inventory, and analytics creates operational inefficiencies and data silos that slow decision-making and stunt growth.

## Approach

- Designed core app architecture around Shopify's App Bridge and Admin API
- Prioritized merchant onboarding to drive time-to-value under 10 minutes
- Built automated workflow triggers tied to Shopify order and customer events
- Collaborated with Shopify's partner team to align on app store listing and merchandising best practices

## Impact

- Successfully listed and approved on the Shopify App Store
- Achieved strong early retention metrics among pilot merchant cohort
- Enabled merchants to reduce manual tasks and reallocate time to growth activities
